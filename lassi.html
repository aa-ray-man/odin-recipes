Sweet Lassi

Description

Lassi is a cooling, refreshing, probiotic drink perfect for the warm summer months made essentially with curd or yogurt, sugar or salt, spices or flavoring ingredients like green cardamom, saffron or rose water. Popular in Punjab and North India, there are many variations of a lassi recipe, including mango lassi, dry fruits lassi, and malai lassi.

Ingredients

  • 500 ml yoghurt (curd)
  • 3 tablespoon sugar
  • salt as required
  • 3 tablespoon chopped mixed dry fruits
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered green cardamom
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Pour curd in a vessel

    First, pour full-fat curd in a pot or vessel. Add sugar and cardamom powder.

  2. Whisk the curd mixture

    Now, whisk the curd mixture using a hand beater. Avoid using an electric one and keep it desi.

  3. Add water to the mixture

    Add water to the mixture and whisk it again for 2 minutes. Make sure you get a nice frothy layer and everything gets blended well.

  4. Garnish it with chopped nuts

    In a long glass, add lassi along with ice cubes. Your Sweet Lassi is ready to serve. Garnish it with chopped nuts. Enjoy this refreshing drink.
